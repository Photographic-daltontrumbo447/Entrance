## Purpose

- hold the compressed hot-root summary for truth ownership, learning, storage, and projection
- keep conflict, evidence, and reconstructability in one semantic trunk

## Confirmed Oracle Points

- Truth substrate distinguishes `Storage Truth -> Cold Memory -> Hot Working Set`.
- `Storage Truth` is the canonical capture plane.
- `Cold Memory` is the curated and conflict-aware memory plane.
- `Hot Working Set` is a reconstructable projection plane rather than a canonical semantic authoring plane.
- Learn landing follows `Storage -> Cold -> Hot projection`; hot-first learning is invalid.
- Hot surfaces own no canonical semantic truth; unreconstructable hot summaries are cache rather than truth.
- External planning mirrors remain captured evidence; imported planning shells do not become Entrance-owned roadmap truth until reconciliation or explicit promotion says so.
- Truth ownership differs by plane; hot owns no semantic truth and cold owns curation outcomes.
- Event-ledger rows are append-only and default storage-first.
- `SIMULATION_EVIDENCE`, its attempt receipt, and its artifact manifest must persist across storage plus cold for reconstructability.
- Concept conflict belongs to truth-system governance rather than runtime machine semantics.
- Concept conflict review and document coherence use separate code families.
- Vector and retrieval indexes are derived search structures rather than a shadow truth plane.
- Engagement profiles may tune surfacing, but not truth-preservation guarantees.
- `PHASE` remains projection while cadence protocol objects land durably in storage plus cold.
